About me

Being an Engineer I always tried to learn programs and tools that could help my career. One day I discovered Unreal Engine 4, where I had my first contact with programming, and I realized that there was no limit to what I could do. A place where I could create new tools and not just become an interpreter of them. Since then, the desire to learn to program has become an obsession.

Anaden Tracker

A web app that helps Another Eden: The Cat Beyond Time and Space's players track of their progress in-game. Every page has a dynamic table rendered with JQuery's plugin DataTables that receives player's input in an efficient way. Currently, it is in development.

Due Gatti

An e-commerce web app for a well-known neighbourhood store. With the beginning of the pandemic, there was a need for innovation as the store had to keep their doors closed. The website was built with Django as back-end and VueJS as front-end, relying on Stripe for online payments.
